Kevin's Bang For A Buck

Between paying tuition, rent, and the abundance of parking tickets I have accumuated, living on a budget is a skill many of us have adapted and mastered at our time here at the University of Michigan. Therefore, I have racked up a list of my favorite meals on a budget, including some restaurants outside of Ann Arbor if we do venture out.

First on the list we have the classic Pancherous on South U. For the price of $8 you can get a large burrito filled to the brim with many of their various offerings, and even as a self-proclaimed glutton I always find myself full to the point of near-nausea when consuming their foods.
